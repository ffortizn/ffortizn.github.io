About me

Francisco is a professional with 20 years of experience in information technologies, background in systems development, implementation administration & improvement.

Francisco has variety of interests like aquaponics, astronomy, bicycle touring, programming, planting trees, technology & vermiculture.

He enjoys reading books, recreative cycling, camping and also loves cats.

In 2016 Francisco rode his bycicle from Mexicali to Los Cabos Baja, since then he dreams* with traveling the world in bicycle; from California to Patagonia first, then... the world!

Francisco is currently part of the team that manufacture the best tortillas in Southern California: Magnolia Foods LLC.

* Kind of... lately is more a plan than a dream.
